Configure Background Apps

The reason to turn off Background Apps is, as a "modern" operating system,
Windows 10 be smart about some background activity without telling you.
Perform any computer task will take some system resources,
Windows need these system resources for its task, but I need the resources, too.
After years of evolution, Windows still not smart enouth to stop its background
activities while I'm using PC for task.
Some of the background activity appears as Store apps, and can block in here.

Open Settings App , select Privacy, then found "Background apps" in the left hand side,
click and switch to Background apps options.
Left the main switch to On, don't turn it off.

I know this seems strange, but due to Windows 10 version 1709,
turn this off will also shut down some of the startup menu features that invisible in this list.
In my case, turn this off and then Search Index will not update new contents.
So in order to prevent background apps, turn all apps on the list to Off,
but keep the apps you wish to allow background activities.

In my case, I have "Settings", "Mail", "Alarms" enabled.
